Our Story

Welcome to Applytics Hub

Applytics Hub is a dynamic platform showcasing an interactive dashboard designed to analyze app trends, rating and user reviews from Google Play Store.
It empowers developers, students and researchers with actionable insights for data-driven decision-making in mobile application development.

Popular Apps Dashboard Features

Creating an Innovative Mobile App Environment with Big Data Analytics

This project aims to enhance the mobile app experience for users and developers. By leveraging big data concepts of Volume, Velocity, and Variety, the rating dashboard analyzes user reviews, app ratings from the Google Play Store and the user behaviours.

The goal is to connect user needs with app development by offering insights to developers, helping users find suitable apps, and advancing the mobile app market.

Dashboard Features

Comprehensive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of key data, offering a quick snapshot of the most important metrics and performance indicators.

Detailed Insights

Breaks down data into deeper, more granular details, allowing users to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies.

Top and Bottom Performers

Highlights the best and worst-performing areas, products, or metrics, helping users focus on what’s working and what needs improvement.

Trends

Shows the progression of data over time, helping to identify patterns, growth, or declines for better forecasting and decision-making.

Interactive Visualization

Engages users with dynamic graphs and charts that allow for exploration and deeper analysis by filtering or zooming into specific data points.
